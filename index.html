SHERIFF WOODY

Sheriff Woody is a fictional character from the Toy Story franchise, created by Pixar Animation Studios. He is the main protagonist of the first three films and serves as the leader of Andy's toys.

Woody is known for his bravery, resourcefulness, and quick thinking. He often serves as the voice of reason among the toys and tries to keep them focused on their ultimate goal, which is to be there for Andy whenever he needs them. Despite his leadership qualities, Woody can also be stubborn and sometimes struggles with his own insecurities.
